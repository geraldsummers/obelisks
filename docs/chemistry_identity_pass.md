# Chemistry Identity Pass

## Summary

This pass makes chemistry a core crafting language instead of a late novelty.
Create handles visible bulk chemistry; PneumaticCraft handles sealed, pressurized,
or gas-driven chemistry. Realistic Ores now have a complete solvent and grinding
media matrix so old deposits gain new identities as chemistry improves.

## Ore Matrix

- Source: `kubejs/server_scripts/40_recipe_add/55_realistic_ores_identity_outputs.js`
- Deposits: 21 current Realistic Ores deposits.
- Solvents: ethanol, acetic acid, sulfuric acid, hydrochloric acid, nitric acid, phosphoric acid.
- Grinding balls: andesite, iron, brass, steel, nickel, titanium, blood-infused, fluix.
- Authored matrix size: 21 x 6 x 8 = 1008 Create mixing recipes.
- Direct ore matrix outputs are existing pack materials only; custom `kubejs:*`
  identity items are downstream crafted components, not direct ore products.

## Solvent Identity

- Ethanol: organic extraction and gentle scouting.
- Acetic acid: weak biological acid, carbonates, safe low-yield recovery.
- Sulfuric acid: bulk sulfide/phosphate and battery chemistry.
- Hydrochloric acid: chloride refining, oxide cleanup, etching identity.
- Nitric acid: oxidizing trace recovery, redstone, precious and fissile chemistry.
- Phosphoric acid: phosphate, bone, fertilizer, ceramic flux, nutrition-adjacent chemistry.

## Ball Identity

Ball retention is defined per solvent and ball. Gentle solvents return more media;
corrosive or oxidizing acids wear media faster. Titanium, nickel, blood-infused,
and fluix balls have distinct survival and output bias rather than being simple
yield upgrades.

The ball textures are dedicated recolors of the vanilla slimeball texture:
`kubejs/assets/kubejs/textures/item/*_grinding_ball.png`.

## Molecule And Casing Use

- Easy molecules begin after Brass: carbon, sulfur, silicon, sodium chloride,
  calcium carbonate, oxides, ethanol, and acetic acid.
- Airtight and later recipes now use chemistry for seals, tubes, etching, circuit
  substrates, batteries, generator bodies, space fabric, and impossible casing prep.
- PNCR board production now consumes copper chloride and silicon dioxide, making
  circuit chemistry explicit.

## Validation

`tools/validate_chemistry_identity.mjs` checks:

- 6 solvents, 8 balls, 21 deposits.
- every solvent/ball retention pair exists.
- the matrix is generated by deposit x solvent x ball loops.
- direct deposit matrix outputs do not use `kubejs:*`.
- all grinding ball models point at dedicated textures.
- all grinding ball textures exist.

The validator is integrated into `node tools/pack_test_suite.mjs`.
